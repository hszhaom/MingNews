About Ming Trends

Ming Trends is an English-language hot trends dashboard focused on overseas traffic sources. We organize fast-moving topics from search, social, video, developer, gaming, entertainment, and product communities into readable rankings and context pages.

Our editorial approach is simple: use platform signals for discovery, add original context, identify uncertainty, attribute sources, and avoid presenting social noise as verified fact. We do not publish misleading, hateful, adult, or harmful content.

What we publish

  • Hot topic rankings adapted for English-speaking global readers.
  • Source guides for Google Trends, Reddit, YouTube, X, Hacker News, Product Hunt, GitHub, TikTok, Steam, Twitch, and related platforms.
  • Original summaries and explainers that turn ranking data into useful context.

Business model

Ming Trends may use display advertising, newsletter sponsorships, and other transparent revenue sources. Advertising does not determine editorial conclusions.
