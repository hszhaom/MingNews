Standards

Editorial Policy

Ming News publishes original English news analysis and explainers. Our goal is to help readers understand important developments with accuracy, context, and clear sourcing.

Accuracy and sourcing

We verify factual claims before publication, distinguish confirmed information from analysis, and avoid presenting speculation as fact. When an article relies on public documents, expert commentary, or published research, we identify the basis for the claim where practical.

Corrections

When we find a material error, we correct it promptly and add an update note when the change affects the article's meaning. Readers can send correction requests through the contact page.

AI assistance

AI tools may be used for research organization, transcription review, outline drafting, grammar suggestions, or technical assistance. Human editors remain responsible for accuracy, editorial judgment, and publication decisions.

Advertising independence

Advertising and sponsorships do not control our editorial conclusions. Sponsored content, if published, will be clearly labeled.

Prohibited content

We do not knowingly publish illegal content, adult sexual content, hate speech, deceptive claims, malware, or content that promotes harm.
