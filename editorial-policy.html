Standards

Editorial Policy

Ming Trends publishes English trend rankings, source guides, and original explainers. Our goal is to help readers understand what is gaining attention across global platforms without confusing popularity signals with verified facts.

Accuracy and sourcing

We distinguish source signals, public discussion, platform rankings, confirmed reporting, and editorial analysis. When a trend is based on social or search activity, we label it as a discovery signal and link to primary sources where practical.

Corrections

When we find a material error, we correct it promptly and add an update note when the change affects the article's meaning. Readers can send correction requests through the contact page.

AI assistance

AI tools may be used for source organization, clustering, outline drafting, grammar suggestions, or technical assistance. Human editors remain responsible for accuracy, attribution, editorial judgment, and publication decisions.

Advertising independence

Advertising and sponsorships do not control our editorial conclusions. Sponsored content, if published, will be clearly labeled.

Prohibited content

We do not knowingly publish illegal content, adult sexual content, hate speech, deceptive claims, malware, or content that promotes harm.
